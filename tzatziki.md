# Tzatziki

Version 1

## Items

* 2 cups Greek Yogurt
* 1/2 cucumber
* 2 Lemons
* 2 Tblsp Dill
* 2 tspn oregano
* 1 tspn kosher salt
* pepper
* 1 tspn garlic
* 1 Tblsp cup rice wine vinegar
* 3 Tblsp olive oil

## Directions

1. Shred cucumber.
2. Toss shredded cucumber with salt.
3. Let sit for 15 minutes.
4. Press out remaining water/juice. Drain.
5. Combine with rest of ingredients.
6. Stir.
